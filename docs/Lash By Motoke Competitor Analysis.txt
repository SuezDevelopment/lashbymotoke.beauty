Key Insights for LashByMotoke.beauty
-----------------------------------
• Training-based beauty studios have stronger customer retention.  
• Booking integration (Fresha, Square, or in-site) builds credibility.  
• Strong visuals (before/after + branding color palette) drive conversions.  
• Offering home service adds local differentiation — highlight it early.  
• Building an educational angle (e.g., “LashByMotoke Academy”) improves SEO & authority.
